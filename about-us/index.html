Fanlei Technology Co., Ltd. is a powerful valve manufacturing company integrating R&D, design, production and sales. We focus on providing high-quality valve products covering a wide range of application needs.
Our leading products include: ductile iron, carbon steel, cast steel, stainless steel series soft-sealed gate valves, butterfly valves, hydraulic control valves, check valves, pipeline valve series, gate valves, stop valves, filters and water hammer flow meters. With advanced technology and excellent design, we are committed to providing customers with reliable valve solutions to ensure efficient and safe operation of the system.

Our Goal

The company will adhere to the business philosophy of “creating high-quality products, customer first, and technology to promote development” and the corporate spirit of “building a business with integrity, developing a business with quality, and promoting morality with virtue”. With first-class quality and reasonable prices, we will work hand in hand with new and old customers to achieve a win-win situation and create brilliance together!
Continuous Innovation
We are committed to ongoing research and innovation to enhance the performance and quality of our valve products, providing customers with the most advanced solutions.
Customer Satisfaction
Our goal is to ensure customer satisfaction through high-quality products and exceptional service, building long-term partnerships with our clients.
Global Expansion
We aim to expand our presence in global markets, delivering our high-quality valve products and services to customers in more countries and regions.
Industry Leadership
Our goal is to become a leader in the valve manufacturing industry, setting benchmarks for excellence in quality and technological innovation, and leading the industry’s growth.

Production Equipment

The company has high-precision CNC machine tools and machining centers, advanced equipment and testing instruments, sophisticated technology and a strict and complete quality management system.
We have introduced advanced design and manufacturing technology and management systems, established a valve technology R&D center, and continuously developed various series of high-tech products. Complete manufacturing equipment and excellent product testing are the guarantee for enterprises to manufacture high-quality products.
