Leading the Valve Industry with Innovation and Quality

Our company is a powerful valve manufacturing enterprise integrating R&D, design, production and sales. The company has advanced design and R&D, mold manufacturing, precision casting, machining and assembly workshops, finished product testing, etc.

The company’s leading products include: ductile iron, carbon steel, cast steel, stainless steel series soft-sealed gate valves, butterfly valves, hydraulic control valves, check valves, pipeline valve series, gate valves, stop valves, filters, water hammer flow meters and other products, and are widely used in metallurgy, mining, petroleum, chemical industry, natural gas power stations, food, paper making, boilers, air conditioning and HVAC, fire protection, water supply and drainage pipelines, environmental protection engineering coal chemical products, municipal administration, construction and other fields.
