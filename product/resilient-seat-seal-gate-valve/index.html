Seal Gate Valve

Rated 0 out of 5
Application Our factory introduces European high-tech valve manufacturing technology to produce elastic seat seal gate valves, which use the overall rubber coating of the gate to produce deformation compensation to achieve a good sealing effect, overcoming the problems of poor sealing, water leakage and rust of general gate valves. It can be widely used as a regulating and intercepting device in fluid pipelines such as tap water, sewage, construction, petroleum, chemical industry, food, medicine, textile, electricity, shipbuilding, metallurgy, energy system, etc. Features
  • Overall rubber coating: The gate adopts overall rubber coating, which has good covering performance and precise geometric dimensions, ensuring reliable sealing and long service life.
  • Light weight: The valve body is made of ductile iron, which is light and easy to install.
  • Flat bottom valve seat: The bottom adopts the same flat bottom valve seat design as the water pipe, which does not produce debris accumulation and has a more reliable seal.
  • Corrosion resistance: The inner cavity is coated with non-toxic epoxy resin to prevent corrosion and rust. It can not only be used for drinking, but also for sewage systems.
  • Three "O" seals: The valve stem adopts three O-ring seals, which has low friction resistance, light switches, and no water leakage.
